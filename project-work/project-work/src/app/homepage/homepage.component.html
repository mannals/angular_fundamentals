Welcome to Project Work!

There are separate pages for a calculator, a text editor and a Super Mario themed quiz!

The calculator can perform adding, subtracting, multiplying and dividing operations. Visually it was made to resemble a real life calculator.

The text editor lets you write text and change the way it looks by bolding, italicizing, and changing its color.

The quiz tests your Super Mario knowledge with three basic questions about the video game franchise.
